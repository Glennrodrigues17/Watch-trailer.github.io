White Building

20-year-old Samnang (Piseth Chhun) and two of his friends, Ah Kha (Chinnaro Soem), and Tol (Sovann Tho) live in the White Building, their lifelong home in Phnom Penh. All three boys practice their dance routine, aspiring to be on a television reality show, while their parents lead a very traditional lifestyle. Their world turns upside down when they hear that the White Building is going to be demolished. Samnang learns that what he always called his home, his stable environment, was always standing on a shaky ground.

No Exit

When recovering addict Darby Throne (Havana Rose Liu) escapes rehab, she lands in some rather unexpected trouble. She’s on her way to Salt Lake City to visit her mother in hospital when she gets stranded in a blizzard and learns of a child’s abduction at the centre she is taking shelter for the night. Now she must find out who the kidnapper is from amongst the other guests, and help rescue the girl in a life-or-death situation.
